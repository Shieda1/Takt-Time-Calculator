What is the takt time calculator?
The takt time calculator is a tool that measures how fast to produce each product in order to meet demand.
How to calculate takt time?
To calculate takt time you should use the following formula:
takt time = (available production time / consumer demand) * 60
Why use takt time calculator?
The takt time calculator is used in order to compute how fast to produce each product in order to meet demand.
